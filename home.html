we are yala

club in batna

Yala Club is a science club founded in 2017 that includes students from all disciplines and is divided into five groups. Three groups according to specialization and two according to work within the club. For specializations: the groups are technology, health, literature and languages. As for work within the club, the groups are: organization and planning, media. The club aspires to make the student possess a leadership thought and enable him to embody his ideas and projects and develop the spirit of teamwork.

media committee

the media committee or in other words,the offecial spokesperson committee of the club,and it is responsible for everything in the media with the help of all existing committees, especially the languages committee,because it is responsible for linguistic auditing,the media committee consists of three branches based on it
the first branch: designe and what is related to the graphicdesigne of its types and forms (advertisements,certificates,invitations...etc.)
the second branch:which is audio and visual,and this means that it is responsible for the flow of images and sounds(videos)in any activity related to the club or anything related to this aspect within the club.
and the third and final branch:which is represented in the movement of social networking sites in all its forms(facebook,instagram,tiktok),in which all aspects of them are covered,including stories,advertisements,comments,posts,and publishing pictures of the activities in which the club is held.so the media committee is the official spokesperson committee for the club by default

planning and sponsoring committee

plunning:1-responsible for organizing the events from setting up the venus.2-organizing attendance.3-maintaining security during the events and others...
sponsoring:responsible for researching sponsorship for the events as peaple who fund the event all the events that the club witnessed,the organizing committee is a part of it always and forever,as all committees in the club depend on the organizing and planning committee
